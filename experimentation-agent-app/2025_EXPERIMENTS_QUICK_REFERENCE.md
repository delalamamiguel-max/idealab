## 💡 Key Insights for New Experiments

### What Works
✅ **Value surfacing at decision point** — Show customers what they get (Converged Reward Card: +4,362%)
✅ **Friction removal** — Simplify the path (PA OE Trial: +104.86%)
✅ **Human-assist escape valve** — Offer help at friction points (CTO for Porters: +18.96%)
✅ **Deep-link + visual redesign** — Combine routing with UX polish (HTP: high conversion)
✅ **Multi-line discount messaging** — Highlight family benefits (Internet HP: +9.7%)

### What Doesn't Work
❌ **Auto-assuming customer context** — Customers want control (Trade-in auto-select: -5.63%)
❌ **Over-engineering simple flows** — 3-step flow doesn't need progress bar (Add-ons: -4.39%)
❌ **Upper-funnel messaging without routing** — Messaging alone doesn't drive conversion (Homepage: -1.24%)
❌ **Low-traffic validation** — Test accessibility on high-traffic pages (Translate: -3.44%)
